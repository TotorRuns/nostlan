  • Cheats
    • How to apply a cheat code
    • 1. Open the Menu
    • 2. Scroll down
    • 3. Click "Cheats"
    • 4. Click "Add new cheat to bottom/top"
    • 5. Click the new cheat that you just created
    • 6. Click "Code"
    • 7. Enter/paste the cheat code
    • 8. When you are done, press enter
    • 9. Click "Enabled" (Set to ON)
    • 10. Go back to Cheats by clicking the bar on the top
    • 11. Click "Apply Changes"
    • 12. Exit the menu
